Our Mission

Provide technology and support to help reduce vaping dependency.

VapeAway is committed to eliminating vaping dependency through the use of its patented technology and a variety of support initiatives. By creating a deeper understanding of the detrimental impacts vaping has on both users and everyone around them, VapeAway plays a vital role in efforts to cease vaping.

Vaping was originally presented as a tool to help people transition away from the detrimental health impacts of smoking combustible cigarettes. Today, many have successfully made the transition from smoking and vaping. While vaping is a better alternative to smoking, staying addicted to vaping is still an unhealthy habit. In addition to those who have switched from smoking to vaping, there are countless first-time vapers, introducing a new generation of people who have never smoked before that became dependent on vaping.

Born out of a father’s resolve to help his son’s addiction, VapeAway created VaporFreeze 2.0, a revolutionary technology. VF 2.0 (VaporFreeze 2.0) powers our VapeAway System, designed for those looking to start their journey to kick the habit, and our VapeAway filters, designed for those vapors who are looking to cut back, both while not introducing additional toxins to their bodies, and allowing a user to continue vaping through a nearly unchanged experience. VapeAway is continuously creating tools and resources such as support groups, educational outreach and community engagement initiatives. VapeAway is the leader in providing critical guidance at every step of a vaper’s journey to quit vaping permanently.
